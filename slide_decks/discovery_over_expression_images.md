## Fair Use Notes for Educational Materials

For a classroom slide deck at a non-profit educational institution, you have broader fair use rights. Generally acceptable:
- Low-resolution images for commentary/criticism
- Screenshots with attribution
- Portion of a work, not the whole

Best practice:
- Include attribution on slides or in speaker notes
- Link to original source when sharing digitally
- Don't redistribute the slide deck commercially
