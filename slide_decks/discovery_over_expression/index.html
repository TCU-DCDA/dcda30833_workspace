The Shift

Expression Discovery
"What do I want to say?" "What can this reveal?"
Artist as author Artist as collaborator
Medium as tool Medium as partner
Outcome predetermined Outcome emergent
Success = intention realized Success = surprise

Why This Matters for Creative Coding

Code is a system.
Systems have behaviors.
Behaviors can surprise you.

A deeper current...

When we encounter something beyond our comprehension—
something that exceeds our capacity to control or predict—
we approach the territory of the Sublime.

Discovery-based art puts us in relation to forces larger than ourselves.
We'll return to this idea later in the semester.

Discussion

In pairs (3 minutes):

  1. Think of a time you made something and were surprised by the result.
  2. What created the surprise? Accident? Constraint? A tool's behavior?
  3. Was the surprise good or bad? Did you keep it?
