## Quick Description

DCDA 30833 introduces students to creative coding as a method for artistic, literary, and critical expression within digital culture. Students use programming to create generative art, interactive poetry, browser-based musical instruments, data-driven creative works, and AI-assisted writing systems. Emphasis is placed on computational thinking, humanities-centered inquiry, creative play, and iterative project development.
